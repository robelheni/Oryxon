Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

We keep this simple: we collect only what we need to run Mekina, we never sell your data, and you can delete your account at any time.

1. Who we are

Mekina is operated by Oryxon Ltd, a technology company registered in England and Wales. We build digital products that make everyday services more accessible.

Questions about this policy: oryxonltd@gmail.com

2. What information we collect

  • Email address — to create and secure your account
  • Name — optional, only if you choose to provide it
  • Password — stored as an encrypted hash, never in plain text
  • Practice activity — questions answered, scores, mock test results, and saved questions
  • Language preference — Amharic, Tigrinya, or English
  • Device session token — to keep you signed in securely on one device at a time

If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and name from Google. We do not receive your Google password. We do not collect your location, contacts, microphone, camera, or any other device data.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show you your practice progress, scores, and history
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, password reset)
  • To keep your account secure (one active session at a time)
  • To improve the app using aggregated, anonymous usage data

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

4. Who we share your data with

  • Railway — cloud hosting and database, EU (Ireland) region
  • Resend — email delivery for verification and password reset emails only
  • Google — only if you choose to sign in with Google

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party.

5. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. Deleting your account removes all your personal data and practice history immediately and permanently.

6. Your rights (UK GDPR)

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Correction — update your name or email via Profile → Manage Account
  • Deletion — delete your account instantly via Profile → Manage Account → Delete account
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to how we process your data

To exercise any right, email oryxonltd@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

7. Security

  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt, never stored in plain text
  • All data transmitted over HTTPS
  • One active session per account at a time
  • Access tokens expire automatically

8. Children

Mekina is for users aged 17 and over (minimum UK driving licence age). If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to this policy

We will notify you by email of any significant changes and update the date above.

10. Contact

Email: oryxonltd@gmail.com
Oryxon Ltd, registered in England and Wales
